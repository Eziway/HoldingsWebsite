Core Products & Accessories

Our comprehensive range is engineered for compatibility with SANS 60076 / SANS 780 transformers across networks from 0.4 kV to 765 kV.

Bushings

Porcelain, composite and resin types with specified creepage (per SANS 780 Table 11) for SA pollution and lightning conditions.

Tap Changers

On-load (OLTC) and off-circuit (OCTC) for voltage regulation, aligned with SANS 60076-1 tapping requirements.

Cooling Systems

Radiators, fans and pumps designed for temperature-rise limits in SANS 60076-2 — suited to high-ambient conditions.

Protection & Monitoring

Buchholz relays, pressure-relief devices, oil/winding temperature indicators and oil-level gauges.

Breathers & Oil Preservation

Silica-gel breathers and filtration systems to manage moisture and temperature cycling.

Valves, Fittings & Insulation

Drain valves, gaskets, seals and pressboard components.

Work Process

01

Enterprise IT Consultation Process

Initial Inquiry & Assessment:
Client reaches out with business requirements.
Consultation & Needs Analysis:
Identify pain points, IT gaps, and objectives.
Solution Design & Proposal:
Provide tailored IT solutions with cost estimates.
Implementation & Integration:
Deploy recommended technologies and integrate with existing systems.
02

End-to-End Development & Testing Process

Requirement Gathering:
Understand client needs, business logic, and system scope.
Planning & Design:
Create system architecture, UI/UX wireframes, and a development roadmap.
Development Phase:
Build software solutions with iterative feedback loops.
Testing & Quality Assurance:
Perform manual and automated testing to ensure security, performance, and scalability.
Deployment & Integration:
Roll out the solution into the client infrastructure.
Maintenance & Updates:
Provide ongoing support, patches, and improvements.
03

Computing Hardware Supply Process

Product Inquiry & Consultation:
Clients browse or request recommendations on hardware solutions.
Quotation & Order Confirmation:
Provide pricing details, bulk discounts, and confirm order details.
Testing & Quality Check:
Ensure all hardware is functional before delivery.
Delivery & Installation:
Ship products and, if needed, assist with installation/setup.
After-Sales Support:
Provide warranty support, troubleshooting, and upgrades.
04

Customer Support & Issue Resolution

Help Desk Ticketing System:
Customers log issues via online portal, email, or phone.
Issue Diagnosis & Troubleshooting:
Identify and resolve problems remotely or on-site.
Replacement or Upgrade (if needed):
Offer hardware swaps or software updates.
Follow-up & Feedback:
Ensure client satisfaction and suggest additional improvements.
